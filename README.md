## Overview
A game of rock, paper, scissors against a computer
<hr>

## Features
* Can play a game of **X points**
* or play **infinitely**!
* Uses random numbers for the computer's choice
* **Hand animations** just like real rock, paper, scissors
* **Hover effects** on buttons, points, and labels
* **footer** including details and contacts of the developer(me)
* **Click animations** for buttons
* disabled input **fades**
* **Choice Selector** to choose from
* **Text box** below the choice selector
* Player's and computer's **points** showed at the bottom
<hr>

## How to Play?

* ### Mode-1 | Limited points game
  * Enter the points of which you want to play the game
  * Click on the button just beside it named **"Enter"**
  * Now start to play by clicking your choice
  * When one round is completed, click the **"Next Round"** button just below the text box
  * When one game is completed, click the **"Reset"** button to go back to the home page
* ### Mode-2 | Infinite points game
  * **Start** without entering any value in the input
  * When one round is completed, click the **"Next Round"** button just below the text box
  * To reset the score, click the **"Reset"** button
